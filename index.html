Team Solo Mid

Team SoloMid (TSM) is a North American eSports Team, created around the community website SoloMid.Net by brothers Andy "Reginald" Dinh and Dan "Dan Dinh" Dinh in September 2009. The team was first seen in early 2011 after being formed to participate in the Riot Season 1 Championship later that year. The organization has also previously hosted the now-disbanded Team SoloMid Evo.

Recent News: Team SoloMid failed to make it out of the group stage at the 2015 World Championship. On October 14th, the team announced the retirement of Dyrus and Santorin, while Lustboy will move from starting Support to Analyst.

Team Liquid

In January 2015, Team Curse was absorbed by Team Liquid (TL), a team based in the Netherlands. On its own, Team Liquid, though new to the League scene, had already made a name for itself in global eSports. Beginning as a StarCraft: Brood War team in 2000, it has since expanded into HearthStone: Heroes of WarCraft and Super Smash Bros. while also maintaining its StarCraft division. Aside from its squads, Team Liquid is known for its website - TeamLiquid.net - which is the most-visited StarCraft-related website in the world.

Team Dignitas

Team Dignitas is an International e-Sports team headquartered in Great Britain. It was founded on September 9th, 2003 as a fusion of the Battlefield 1942 clans Legion Condor and Sweden Kompanix. Later on, more teams were added to the organization, and in July 2004 Team Dignitas was registered as a company - Team Dignitas Ltd. In addition to their League of Legends team, Dignitas also sponsors teams and players for Battlefield 3, Counter Strike, Fifa, various fighting games, Quake Live, racing games, and StarCraft II. Michael "ODEE" O'Dell is the current Managing Director of the Team Dignitas organization. The League of Legends division of Dignitas is one of the leading teams in the North American scene, placing highly in numerous premier and major events, both regional and international, since its conception with the acquisition of Rock Solid in September 2011. Currently, Team Dignitas hosts one team in the North American Season 4 League Championship Series

Recent News: November 18th, SmittyJ , Kirei , and Apollo Join the starting lineup

Cloud 9

Cloud9 (C9) is a North American League of Legends team competing in the North American League Championship Series. The team is notable for winning two LCS seasons, as well as a top eight finish at the Season 3 World Championship. The team currently competes under the name Cloud9 G2A, in representation of their sponsor G2A.com. It was initially formed by the roster of Quantic Gaming after leaving the financially struggling Quantic Gaming organization and currently also features divisions in DotA 2, Hearthstone: Heroes of WarCraft, CS:GO, Super Smash Bros. Melee, Halo and Heroes of the Storm.
